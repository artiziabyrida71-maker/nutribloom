What is Phool Makhana?

Phool Makhana, also known as fox nuts or lotus seeds, are the seeds of the Euryale ferox plant that grows in ponds and wetlands across India. These seeds have been a staple in Ayurvedic medicine and Indian cuisine for centuries.

At Pure Phool Makhana, we source these seeds directly from farmers who follow traditional harvesting methods and modern quality standards to ensure you get the purest, most nutritious fox nuts available.

Delicious Recipes

Makhana Kheer

Classic Indian dessert made with fox nuts, milk and aromatic spices

Makhana Kheer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Pure Phool Makhana
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • 10-12 saffron strands
  • 2 tbsp chopped nuts

Method:

  1. Dry roast makhana until crisp, then coarsely grind
  2. Boil milk with saffron till reduced slightly
  3. Add sugar and cardamom powder
  4. Add crushed makhana and simmer for 5 minutes
  5. Garnish with nuts and serve chilled

Roasted Makhana

Crunchy, spicy snack perfect for healthy munching

Roasted Makhana Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Pure Phool Makhana
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1/2 tsp black salt
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp chaat masala

Method:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan on low flame
  2. Add makhana and roast for 5-7 minutes stirring constantly
  3. Turn off heat and sprinkle all spices
  4. Mix well and store in airtight container
  5. Enjoy within 5-7 days
